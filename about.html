ABOUT US

Aizen Recruitment is a one stop location for companies and jobseeker’s alike; networking between our vast databases of companies who are looking for a new employee, or by building our list of vetted jobseekers and helping them to find the best position for their experience. We are a thoughtful company and take the time to hear each of our client’s need. Whether you are a company seeking to expand or needing to fill an available role, or someone who finds themselves in the employment market again (or even for the first time), we take a humanistic approach with all of our work. Listening to our client’s needs, learning how they see their next position or what type of candidate would be suit them, and following through with the client in mind at all times.

We pride ourselves in discovering the best candidates for any position and can help jobseekers build their careers by offering guidance and helping them to link with their next position.

Aizen Recruitment has an international client portfolio, and as such we are able to draw from a wide range of candidates who hold distinctly different skillsets.

For companies, we offer a guarantee in the placement of any candidate we propose and can replace a candidate where one doesn’t fit the role after they start. In this way companies can know that Aizen Recruitment can offer several candidates for any position and find the one that best fits the company and the employer’s needs. We understand the challenges that companies face in finding the right employee, and understand the challenges faced by jobseekers in finding the right role. We act as a conduit for both to find each other and our experience in human resources and recruitment allows us to make considered recommendations to both types of clients in identifying the proper job or employee, as required.

We are a dedicated company and our mission is to link the best people to the best roles.
